Welcome to premium hotel ,a luxury hotel that offers an old-world charm and a harmonious blend of contemporary elegance and a stunning view of the sea. The hotel overlooks the tropical garden and the sea with a beautiful beach of over 160m. It has an african style building with the characteristic of makuti roofs, the arab constructions that contrast with the blue of the sky and the villas on the beach with their rooms with sea view. From luxury accomodation to various types of cuisines, an aesthetically pleasing garden restaurant, a bar and first-class event facilities are all offered in our hotel.
